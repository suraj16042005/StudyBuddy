Frequently Asked Questions

Excel Coins are our in-platform currency used to book sessions with mentors, access premium courses, and participate in exclusive workshops. They offer a flexible way to pay for your learning needs.

We offer three tiers: Stater, Premium, and Bulk. Each package provides a certain number of Excel Coins, along with bonus coins and varying levels of discounts on sessions. Premium and Bulk packages are subscription-based, offering continuous benefits.

The Stater package is a one-time purchase of coins. The Premium and Bulk packages are subscription-based, meaning you'll receive coins and benefits regularly (e.g., monthly) as long as your subscription is active, ensuring you always have coins for your learning journey.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for your initial purchase if you are not satisfied. For subscription plans, you can cancel anytime, and your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing cycle. Please refer to our Terms of Service for detailed refund policies.

Bonus coins are automatically added to your balance upon purchase. Discounts on 1-on-1 and group sessions are applied automatically when you book a session, based on your active subscription plan.
